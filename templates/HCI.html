HCI Principle

Here you can find out about how we improved our design

We held discussions with a number of aproximatly 10-15 of our colleagues (as well as with our client) both in the early stages and in late stages of the project in order to get a better idea of how to improve our design (including the functional part, but especially the user interface). This was very useful as we came back a large number of times over the design and improved things.

The largest number of improvements after holding these talks with our colleagues were on the UX side of the project (chaing the design of certain pages), but some of them were on the functional part as well (it helped us finalise our MoSCoW type requirements list).
