What Do We Do?

Equity Crowdfunding

We believe that small businesses have the potential to change the world. This is why we help companies get the funding they need to unleash their full potential.

Stocks

We enable small and big investors to buy stocks as opposed to directly found the companies. With Ammoenio anyone can become an investor and play an active role in the growth of the projects they believe in.

Security

We use Blockchain technology to ensure that every transaction is protected by the highest level of security.
