Welcome the future of investing!


What do we do?

Equity Crowdfunding

We believe that start-ups and small businesses have the potential to change the world. This is why we help companies get the funding they need through share selling in order to unleash their full potential.

Shares

With Ammoenio anyone can become an investor. By enabling investors to buy shares into the companies, they can fund the projects they believe in, follow them through their design lifetime and see them getting succesful on the market.

Divident Payments

By using Ammoenio, not only do investors fund their dearest projects, but they also revel in its success. The more shares they buy, the more dividents they get.


Track Share Value

Ammoenio offers investors mechanism to track the market value of their shares and take the best decissions based on how well the company is doing on the market.

Share Trading

With Ammoenio, investors can take advantage of the volatily of the market. They can buy shares at low cost, sell them when their cost rises and keep the profit.

Voting on Decissions

All investors, big and small, can contribute to the growth of their project by taking important decissions and voting in company decissions.




Why use Ammoenio?

Security

We use the Ethereum Blockchain hyperledger and the latest security solutions on the market such as SSL certificates and Cloudflare routing in order to ensure that every transaction is protected by the highest level of security.

Anonymity

Because all transactions happen in the blockchain, we do not keep any historic of transactions or private information. Idenities of investors are kept private from other users (but will still be available to companies people invest in and to tax authorities).

Speed

We make use of a combination of Digital Ocean's fast SSD hosting and Cloudflare's high quality DNS routing and super-fast caching to secure the fastest access to Ammoenio for our customers.
